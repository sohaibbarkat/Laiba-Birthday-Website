Paigham · A Letter For You

7 July, Ek Mehnati Rooh Ka Jashn

Laiba, aaj ka din sirf ek tareekh nahi — yeh us simplicity, sincerity aur discipline ka jashn hai jo tum har roz khamoshi se jeeti ho. Duniya shor machati hai, dikhawe mein jeeti hai — aur tum chup chaap apni kitabon ke saath baith kar apna kal likhti ho.

Tumhari simplicity mein ek khaamoshi hai jo bohot kam logon mein hoti hai — na shikwa, na dikhawa, sirf nayyat aur niyat ki safai. Tumhari sincerity har us insaan ko mehsoos hoti hai jo tumhare qareeb aata hai. Aur tumhari discipline, woh subah ki azaan se shuru hoti hai aur raat ke aakhri pehar tak chalti hai.

Har raat jab duniya so jati hai, tum jaagti rehti ho — kitaab khol kar, khwaab ko qareeb laate hue. Yeh mehnat zaaya nahi jaati, Laiba. Yeh wohi mehnat hai jo ek din "Dr Laiba Siddique" ke naam ko izzat dilayegi.

Aaj, hum sirf tumhari birthday nahi mana rahe — hum us future doctor ko salaam kar rahe hain jo tum banne wali ho. Happy Birthday, hardworking soul. 🤍
